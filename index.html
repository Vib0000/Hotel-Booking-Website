Room-Types

Family Room

A family room is a larger accommodation option suitable for families or groups. It typically consists of multiple beds, providing ample space for parents and children to stay together comfortably. Family rooms may include additional amenities such as a seating area, television, and a larger bathroom. They offer convenience and affordability for families traveling together.

Single Occupancy Room

A single occupancy room is designed for one guest. It typically features a single bed and basic amenities such as a desk, chair, wardrobe, and an en-suite bathroom. These rooms are ideal for solo travelers or business professionals looking for privacy and comfort during their stay.

Duplex Room

A duplex room is a two-story accommodation featuring separate living and sleeping areas. The lower level often includes a living room, kitchenette, and dining area, while the upper level houses the bedroom and bathroom. Duplex rooms offer extra space and privacy, making them ideal for extended stays or travelers seeking a more luxurious experience.
